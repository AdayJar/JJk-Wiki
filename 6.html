Jujutsu Kaisen season 3: release date, trailer, and everything we know so far

"Jujutsu Kaisen" is a popular Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami.
The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who joins a secret organization of jujutsu sorcerers to eliminate a powerful curse.

Season 3 is expected to adapt the Culling Games arc, building on the intense storyline after the Shibuya Incident arc.

Main Info

Jujutsu Kaisen season 3 will adapt the Culling Games arc, following events from season 2’s Shibuya Incident.

The Prison Realm

A key element in the Shibuya Incident arc, the Prison Realm is where Gojo was sealed away.

The Culling Games

The Culling Games arc, a battle royale, will be a central focus in season 3.

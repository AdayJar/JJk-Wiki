Info

"Jujutsu Kaisen" is a widely acclaimed Japanese manga series created by Gege Akutami, which has gained significant popularity both in Japan and internationally.
The story is set in a world where curses, born from human malice and negative emotions, threaten the lives of innocent people.
The narrative primarily follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student with exceptional physical abilities and a kind heart.
